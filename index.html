FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Why does Windows show a security warning?

Windows SmartScreen shows warnings for new applications that haven't built up reputation yet. This is normal for independent software. The app is completely safe‚Äîit stores everything locally, doesn't connect to the internet, and collects no data. Click "More info" then "Run anyway" to proceed.

Does it work with other PDF readers?

Currently, the tool is optimized specifically for Adobe Acrobat and Adobe Reader. Support for other PDF readers may be added in future updates.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. All screenshots are saved locally to your computer. The app doesn't connect to the internet or send any data anywhere. Your documents stay completely private.

Will it slow down my computer?

No. The tool is lightweight and only activates when Adobe Acrobat is in focus. It uses minimal system resources and runs quietly in the background.

What image format are screenshots saved in?

Screenshots are saved as PNG files for optimal quality. File names include the document name and timestamp for easy organization.

Can I use this for compliance and auditing?

Yes! The tool is perfect for creating audit trails, documenting form completion, and maintaining records for compliance requirements. Each screenshot is timestamped.
