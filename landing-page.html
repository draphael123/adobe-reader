Legal Stuff

Privacy Policy

TL;DR: We Don't Collect Anything

Seriously. This app runs entirely on your computer. We have no servers. We don't see your screenshots. We don't know you exist. It's actually a bit lonely for us.

The Longer Version (for lawyers)

The only network request is an optional check for updates, which pings GitHub's API. You can disable this. We don't track you, we don't analyze your usage, we don't sell your data (because we don't have any).

Your Screenshots

Stored locally on YOUR computer, in a folder YOU choose. We couldn't access them even if we wanted to (which we don't).

Open Source = Trust

Don't believe us? Read the entire source code. It's all there. We literally cannot hide anything.
