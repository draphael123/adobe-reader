Features

Everything You Need

A focused tool that does one thing exceptionally well.

Automatic Capture

Screenshots are taken automatically when you scroll or navigate to a new page in your PDF.

Organized by Document

Each PDF gets its own folder, keeping your captures organized and easy to find.

Smart Detection

Duplicate pages are automatically skipped to save storage space.

Runs in Background

Sits quietly in your system tray, capturing pages while you work.

Customizable

Choose PNG or JPEG format, set quality, and configure capture behavior.

Manual Hotkey

Press Ctrl+Shift+S anytime to capture the current view manually.

How It Works

Three Simple Steps

Get started in under a minute.

1

Download & Install

Run the installer and follow the simple setup wizard. No complex configuration needed.

2

Open Your PDF

Open any PDF document in Adobe Acrobat Reader or Pro. The app detects it automatically.

3

Navigate & Capture

Simply scroll or use Page Down. Screenshots are captured and saved automatically.
