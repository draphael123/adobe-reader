Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PDF Screenshot Tool ("we", "our", or "the application") handles your data and information.

TL;DR: We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your data. Everything stays on your computer. The only internet connection is for checking updates (which you can disable).

1. Data Collection

We collect nothing.

PDF Screenshot Tool operates entirely on your local machine. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Send data to external servers
  • Access your PDF content
  • Monitor your browsing or other activities

2. Screenshots

All screenshots are saved locally on your computer in the folder you specify. We never:

  • Access the content of your screenshots
  • Transmit screenshots to any server
  • Store screenshots in the cloud
  • Share screenshots with third parties

Your screenshots remain completely private and under your control.

3. Internet Connectivity

The application has one optional internet connection:

  • Update checking: The app can check GitHub for new versions. This is optional and can be disabled in settings.

When checking for updates, the app only:

  • Connects to GitHub's public API
  • Reads public release information
  • Does not send any personal data

4. Local Storage

The application stores the following data locally on your computer:

  • Configuration: Your settings (save folder, capture options, etc.) stored in %APPDATA%\PDFScreenshotTool\config.json
  • Statistics: Local usage statistics (capture counts, document names) stored in %APPDATA%\PDFScreenshotTool\stats.json
  • Logs: Application logs for troubleshooting stored in %APPDATA%\PDFScreenshotTool\app.log

All of this data remains on your computer and is never transmitted anywhere.

5. Third-Party Services

PDF Screenshot Tool does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Open Source

This application is open source. You can review the source code at:

https://github.com/draphael123/adobe-reader

You can verify for yourself that we're not collecting or transmitting any data.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to request, delete, or modify. All your data (screenshots, settings, statistics) is stored locally on your computer and can be deleted at any time by:

  • Deleting the application folder
  • Removing files from %APPDATA%\PDFScreenshotTool\
  • Uninstalling the application

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/draphael123/adobe-reader/issues
  • Website: https://pdfscreenshottool.com
